About Us
EST. FEBRUARY 23, 1975

50 Years of Service
& Leadership

The Rotaract Club of WCE Sangli is among the oldest and most dynamic clubs of the college, proudly celebrating its 50th Charter Year.

Guided by the spirit of "Service Above Self" and our motto "Learning Is Our Precept," we bring together passionate individuals dedicated to community service, personal growth, and leadership development.

Through diverse projects and creative initiatives, our members strive to make a meaningful impact on society while nurturing themselves into the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Pillars

Vision & Mission

Four avenues of service that define everything we do — from community outreach to international understanding.

🌍

Community Service

Health camps, food donation drives, and awareness campaigns addressing local societal needs. Events like Dirghayu and Swayamseva bring meaningful change to the community.

🚀

Professional Dev

Fostering skills through personality development meets, mock interviews, and placement-ready programs. Helping members tap diverse career options and grow holistically.

🌐

International Service

Promoting international understanding through the mega event Global Mandate. Enhancing bonds with Rotaract clubs worldwide and encouraging members to engage with global issues.

⚡

Club Service

Building team spirit through treks, outings, General Body Meets, and Wonder Pulse. Bringing members together with highly contagious Rotaract energy in every phase of life.

Our Journey

50 Years of Legacy

1975
Club Chartered

Rotaract Club of WCE Sangli officially chartered on February 23rd — one of India's earliest collegiate Rotaract clubs.

1990s
Community Roots Deepen

Regular health camps, food drives, and social awareness campaigns become annual traditions across Sangli district.

2000s
Global Mandate Born

The flagship International Service event "Global Mandate" is launched, connecting WCE Rotaract to clubs worldwide.

2022
Club Founded

Club founded with a vision of innovation — combining Rotaract's service ethos with technology leadership for modern youth.

2023
First Tech Event

Organised the first technology-driven event, showcasing members' skills and establishing the chapter's technical identity.

2025
50th Charter Year 🎉

Celebrating five golden decades. 500+ members strong, the club embarks on its jubilee year with renewed energy and bold ambitions.
